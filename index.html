Huperzine A

**Huperzine A** is derived from the Chinese club moss plant and is recognized for its potential in treating Alzheimer's disease. It works by increasing levels of neurotransmitters in the brain, which play a crucial role in memory and learning processes.

L-Glutamine

**L-Glutamine** is an amino acid that serves multiple functions in the body. While it's essential for immune system support, it also plays a role in brain function and digestion. As a neurotransmitter in the brain, it aids in concentration and mental alertness.

St. John’s Wort

**St. John’s Wort** is a herb traditionally used for its mood-enhancing properties. It's often employed as a natural remedy for depression and anxiety. Additionally, it supports brain health by facilitating oxygen delivery and regulating stress hormones.
5

Phosphatidylserine Complex

**Phosphatidylserine Complex** is a phospholipid that plays a vital role in maintaining the health and integrity of cell membranes, especially in the brain. It's known to improve memory, cognition, and mood in the elderly and those with cognitive decline.
6

Ginkgo Biloba

**Ginkgo Biloba** is one of the oldest tree species on earth and has been used in traditional medicine for millennia. Its leaves are known to improve blood flow to the brain, enhancing cognitive functions like memory, focus, and processing speed.
7

N-Acetyl L-carnitine

**N-Acetyl L-carnitine**, also known as NALCAR, is an amino acid derivative that supports brain health. It aids in energy production within mitochondria, the powerhouses of cells, and has been linked to improved cognitive function, especially in older adults.
8

L-carnitine

**L-carnitine** is an amino acid that plays a pivotal role in energy production. In the context of brain health, it helps in the production of neurotransmitters essential for memory and thought processes.
9

Phosphatidylserine

**Phosphatidylserine** is a fatty substance that covers and protects every cell in the body and is involved in the clotting function of blood. It's particularly vital for the proper functioning of nerve cells within the brain, ensuring the efficient transmission of signals.
10

Ginkgo Biloba Leaf

**Ginkgo Biloba Leaf** extract is renowned for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. It improves blood circulation, especially in the cerebral region, and is believed to protect against age-related issues like cognitive decline and memory loss.
