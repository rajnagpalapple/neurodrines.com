Here is a list of major Neurodrine Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits: 

1

Bacopa Monnieri

Bacopa Monnieri is a traditional herb that has been used for centuries in Ayurvedic medicine. It is well-regarded for its potential to enhance cognitive function, particularly memory and learning capabilities. Modern research has begun to explore its effects on the brain, including its ability to increase certain brain chemicals involved in thinking, learning, and memory.
2

Huperzine A

Huperzine A is a compound extracted from the herbs of the Huperziceae family and has been studied for its potential in improving cognitive functions and protecting the brain against agents that can impair function. It operates as an acetylcholinesterase inhibitor, which means it helps prevent the breakdown of acetylcholine, an important neurotransmitter associated with memory and learning.
3

St. John's Wort

St. John's Wort is commonly known for its antidepressant effects and is used in various supplements for mental health. Beyond its mood-regulating benefits, it is believed to contribute to cognitive health by protecting the brain from oxidative stress and promoting a balanced mental state, which can be beneficial for maintaining cognitive functions.
4

Ginkgo Biloba

Ginkgo Biloba is one of the oldest living tree species, and its extracts are used for their cognitive-enhancing effects. It is thought to improve cognitive function by promoting good blood circulation in the brain and protecting the brain from neuronal damage. It has been widely studied for its potential benefits in improving memory and attention in both healthy individuals and those with cognitive impairment.
