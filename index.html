Here is a list of major Neurodrine Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits:

Bacopa Monnieri

**Bacopa Monnieri** is a traditional herb often used in Ayurvedic medicine. It's renowned for its cognitive-enhancing properties, particularly in improving memory and reducing anxiety. Its active compounds, bacosides, are believed to protect brain cells and combat oxidative stress, leading to better mental clarity and focus.

Huperzine A

**Huperzine A** is derived from the Chinese club moss plant and is known for its potential in boosting levels of neurotransmitters in the brain. It's often used to enhance memory and learning capabilities, and it may also offer protection against age-related cognitive decline.
3

L-Glutamine

**L-Glutamine** is an amino acid that plays a crucial role in brain health. It serves as a primary source of energy for brain cells, ensuring they function optimally. Additionally, L-Glutamine is believed to improve concentration and mental alertness.
4

St. John’s Wort

**St. John’s Wort** is a plant with a long history of use for its mood-enhancing properties. It's often employed to combat symptoms of depression and anxiety. Its active compounds, hypericin and hyperforin, are believed to have neuroprotective effects and may also enhance neurotransmitter activity.
5

Phosphatidylserine

**Phosphatidylserine** is a phospholipid that's vital for cognitive function. It's present in the cell membranes of the brain and plays a role in cell-to-cell communication. Supplementation with phosphatidylserine has been linked to improved memory, mood, and cognitive function.
6

Ginkgo Biloba

**Ginkgo Biloba** is an ancient tree species with leaves that have been used for millennia for their medicinal properties. It's known to improve blood flow to the brain, which can enhance cognitive function, memory, and concentration. Additionally, it has antioxidant properties that protect the brain from oxidative damage.
7

Acetylcarnitine (N-Acetyl L-carnitine)

**Acetylcarnitine (N-Acetyl L-carnitine)** is an amino acid derivative that's crucial for energy production in cells. In the context of brain health, it's believed to enhance cognitive function, reduce mental fatigue, and protect against age-related cognitive decline.
8

Vinpocetine

**Vinpocetine** is a synthetic compound derived from the periwinkle plant. It's known to increase blood flow to the brain, enhancing oxygen and nutrient delivery. This can lead to improved cognitive function, memory, and alertness.
9

Vinpocetine

**Vinpocetine** (mentioned twice in the list) is a synthetic derivative of the periwinkle plant's alkaloid vincamine. It's often used to enhance memory and cognition by increasing blood flow to the brain and promoting better oxygen utilization.
10

Phosphatidylserine Complex

**Phosphatidylserine Complex** is a combination of phospholipids essential for maintaining the health and integrity of cell membranes, especially in the brain. It supports cognitive functions, including memory and learning, by facilitating cell-to-cell communication and ensuring optimal neurotransmitter activity.
