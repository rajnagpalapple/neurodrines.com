Neurodrine Ingredients

Bacopa Monnieri

**Bacopa Monnieri**: A traditional herb often used in Ayurvedic medicine, Bacopa Monnieri is renowned for its cognitive-enhancing properties. It's believed to improve memory, reduce anxiety, and have antioxidant effects that protect the brain from oxidative stress.

Huperzine A

**Huperzine A**: Derived from the Chinese club moss plant, Huperzine A acts as a cognitive enhancer. It's primarily known for its ability to inhibit an enzyme that degrades the learning neurotransmitter, thus increasing alertness and protecting nerve cells.
3

Ginkgo Biloba

**Ginkgo Biloba**: An ancient plant extract, Ginkgo Biloba is widely recognized for its ability to support brain health. It promotes better blood circulation in the brain and is believed to protect against age-related cognitive decline.
4

St. John’s Wort

**St. John’s Wort**: Often used as a natural remedy for depression, St. John’s Wort has properties that can help balance mood and neurotransmitters in the brain. It's also believed to have neuroprotective qualities.
5

L-Glutamine

**L-Glutamine**: An essential amino acid, L-Glutamine plays a crucial role in brain health. It serves as a building block for proteins and aids in neurotransmitter function, ensuring optimal cognitive performance.
6

Phosphatidylserine

**Phosphatidylserine**: A type of fat compound primarily found in the brain, Phosphatidylserine plays a pivotal role in maintaining cellular function, especially in neurons. It's believed to support memory and cognitive function.
7

Acetylcarnitine

**Acetylcarnitine**: Often referred to as ALCAR, Acetylcarnitine is an amino acid naturally produced in the body. It's known to help produce energy and is believed to have neuroprotective benefits, especially in terms of memory and mood.
8

Vinpocetine

**Vinpocetine**: Derived from the periwinkle plant, Vinpocetine is known to enhance blood flow to the brain. This increased circulation is believed to improve cognitive functions like memory and attention.
9

N-Acetyl L-carnitine

**N-Acetyl L-carnitine**: A variant of L-Carnitine, N-Acetyl L-carnitine is involved in cellular metabolism and energy production. It's believed to have neuroprotective properties and is often used to address cognitive decline.
10

Phosphatidylserine Complex

**Phosphatidylserine Complex**: This complex is crucial for cognitive function, aiding in cell-to-cell communication in the brain. It's believed to support memory, learning, and concentration, making it a vital component for brain health.
